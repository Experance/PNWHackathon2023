About Page

This extension allows you to organize websites you encounter. Currently, there are 5 pre-built folders and they are Coding, School, Social Media, Entertainment, and Miscellaneous. To add/remove links to/from folders, you can either use this webpage (click on the "Add/Remove Websites") tab or right click and add/remove from the Linkpiler context menu. Unfortunately, this extension does not currently supported custom folders. That functionality will be added in future versions.
